Everything's Vulnerable

Even the strongest systems are managed by vulnerable people. This is my daily reminder to keep going
as an ethical hacker. We don't only hack systems; we hack people too because humans are the key to
breaking the strongest systems. Note that everything can be hacked including people; all you need
is a proper exploit, and the vulnerability will call you by name.
